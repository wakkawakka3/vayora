About Vayora

Vayora is a simple, beautiful weather app that lets you check real-time weather for any city in the world. We believe weather information should be quick, easy, and accessible to everyone.

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, Vayora uses the OpenWeatherMap API to deliver accurate weather data instantly. No bloat, no complexity—just the information you need.
